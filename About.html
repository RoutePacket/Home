About RoutePacket

Empowering the next generation of Network Engineers through hands‑on learning, real‑world labs & industry‑backed guidance.

Who We Are

RoutePacket is a modern platform dedicated to helping students, beginners, and working professionals excel in **Network Engineering**. We bring together real-world interview preparation, hands‑on labs, troubleshooting scenarios, and curated networking content — all simplified and practical.

Our Journey

RoutePacket was founded in 2023 with a very clear mission:

“To make Networking education practical, accessible, and aligned with real industry needs.”

We noticed that most learners rely on scattered PDFs or outdated tutorials. We wanted to change that. Our team started building structured content, interactive labs, and modern interview resources tailored for the current IT landscape.

Today, RoutePacket has helped learners prepare for roles such as:

  • Network Support Engineer
  • NOC Engineer
  • Routing & Switching Engineer
  • SD‑WAN / Firewall Engineer
  • Systems & Cloud Networking roles

What We Believe

  • Learning should be **practical**, not memorized.
  • Labs should simulate **real enterprise networks**.
  • Interview guidance should come from **actual industry scenarios**.
  • Documentation should be **clean, modern, and accessible**.
  • Every beginner should get an equal chance to grow.

Our Moto

“Learn, Lab, Level‑Up.”

Everything we create is built around these three core values.

Why RoutePacket?

  • Industry‑oriented interview questions & solutions.
  • Hands‑on labs with Packet Tracer, GNS3 & EVE‑NG.
  • Modern and clean web‑based learning (no PDFs).
  • Books, merch & specialized networking tools.
  • 24/7 learning access — anytime, anywhere.
